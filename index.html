Why does H₂ win? Temperature measures average kinetic energy (½mv²), not speed. Since H₂ is 14× lighter than N₂, it must travel ~√14 ≈ 3.7× faster to hold the same energy — so heat diffuses across the chamber far quicker. Switch gases and watch the difference.
